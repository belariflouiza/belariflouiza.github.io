Master in Full Stack Development

Program Overview

The Master's program in Full Stack Development trains professionals capable of mastering the entire application development chain. This includes back-end development using server technologies (PHP, JEE, .NET, NODEJS, REST, etc.) and front-end development, which deals with the user interface and experience (HTML, CSS, JS, UX, etc.). Our graduates will be versatile, adapting to challenges presented by companies and evolving technologies. This versatility is crucial for integration into a diverse local job market with varying needs and technologies.

See RNCP File

Access Conditions

  • M1: Holders of a bac +3 in computer science or an equivalent diploma.
  • M2: Automatic for holders of the M1 DFS and based on application for other candidates.
